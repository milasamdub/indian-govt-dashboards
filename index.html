Keywords for Indian government dashboards

Real time

State dashboards perform their liveness, as if the metrics of development were a measure of time. Yet digging deeper, you find that some of these are last month's numbers, some are from six months ago, some are even a year old.

Leapfrogging

The idea that developing nations can "skip" stages of development.

(reference: Times of India)

Competition

Centralising information, dashboards enable the comparison of departments, districts or municipalities against each other. They do this by assigning star ratings, calculating composite scores and assigning ranks, or. This comparison is imagined as a means of creating competition.

(reference: NITI Aayog 2018)
