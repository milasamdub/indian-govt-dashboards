Keywords for Indian government dashboards

Real time

State dashboards perform their liveness, as if the metrics of development were a measure of time. But if you dig deeper, you find that some of these are last month's numbers, some are from six months ago, some are even a year old.

Leapfrogging

The idea that developing nations can "skip" stages of development, as if history was linear and technology could take you from 3 to 8.

(reference: Economic Times)

Competition

Centralising information, dashboards enable the comparison of departments, districts or municipalities against each other. They do this by assigning star ratings, calculating composite scores and assigning ranks, or through heatmaps. According to the proponents of dashboards, this comparison is imagined as a means of leveraging competition to make government more efficient.

(reference: NITI Aayog 2018)

Extraction

The techno-utopian principles embodied by state dashboards legitimise the extraction of data and other heavier resources.
